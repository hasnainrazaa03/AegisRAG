# Structural Tolerance Limits for Primary Load-Bearing Beams

## Overview
This document outlines the standard structural tolerance limits for primary load-bearing beams used in Class A commercial aerospace engineering projects.

## Material Specifications
The primary beams are constructed from Aerospace-Grade Titanium Alloy Ti-6Al-4V.

## Tolerance Limits
- Maximum Tensile Strength: 950 MPa
- Yield Strength Limit: 880 MPa
- Maximum Load Capacity: 15,000 kg per beam
- Deflection Limit: 1/360 of the span under maximum load

## Safety Factors
A safety factor of 1.5 must be applied to all calculations involving primary load-bearing beams. The structural tolerance limits must not be exceeded during peak operational stress testing.
